About Us

We at RationDot , use blockchain technology for a decentralized, distributed ledger that enables secure and transparent record-keeping. We have created a permanent, tamper-proof record of transactions that can be publicly accessed and verified by all participants in the network



Transparency

This system will provide transparency among all the stakeholders as all the transactions/processes will be happening on a public blockchain network

Reduced Corruption

The use of secure digital signature and smart contracts will also lead to reduce corruption during the distribution process as everything will be noted down on a distributed ledger

User involvement

Users will have the power and control to sign the transaction that they have received the required amount of food grains that has been issued by the government
